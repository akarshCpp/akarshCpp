## About Me
I am a B.Tech Computer Science student specializing in Big Data Analytics at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, with a strong interest in Machine Learning, Data Science, and Artificial Intelligence. I focus on building data-driven solutions to real-world problems and have experience working with technologies like Python, SQL, PyTorch, TensorFlow, and FastAPI. My work involves developing end-to-end machine learning systems, from data processing and feature engineering to model building and deployment. I am particularly interested in deep learning, scalable AI systems, and applying data science techniques to practical use cases, while continuously expanding my knowledge in advanced machine learning and MLOps.
